4 Steps to Apply

1. Submit form: This allows us to see if we still have space for your child and if not, put their names on our waiting list. Once you have filled out the form, we can offer you the opportunity for a private school tour. Submit pre-registration form here.

2. Visit us: Choosing a school for your child is a big decision. We encourage all families to visit us first to see if our school is the right place for your child.

3. Submit real application: Complete the application form and add the supporting documents. Download the form here

4. Pay tuition and fully refundable deposit: To secure your place, you then make payment consisting of one-month tuition plus two months of a fully refundable deposit. We don’t charge any application fees, registration fees, or other expenses. Bank: Krungsri 4740006063

Tuition Fees with no surprises

  • You pay tuition monthly
  • Tuition includes snack, healthy lunch, toiletries and all other materials
  • There are no application or registration fees
  • Our security deposit is fully refundable
  • Prices include VAT
  • Extended Care is available on request

Terms that make sense

We separate our terms in three trimesters, without long breaks in between. That means that we are open many more days than traditional international schools and can provide young children more stability in their daily routines. Plus, there are no additional fees for summer camps or their like.

Scholarships

Not every family is fortunate enough to be able to afford excellent education for their children. We, therefore, offer scholarships to up to 10% of our student body.

We base these scholarships entire on the parents’ motivation and interest in our philosophy and not on the abilities of the child. If you are interested in a scholarship for your child, please send us an email in English or Thai to hello@sprouts.co.th including the following details:

  • A motivation letter
  • A collection of images of your daily routines
  • Family financials (income, bank statements)
  • Application Form with required documents
  • Attending at least three Sprouts Playgroups is highly recommended
