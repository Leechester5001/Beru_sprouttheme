Our Approach

We focus on the holistic development of each individual child, following the evidenced-based early childhood education found in modern Kindergartens in Germany.

Through play, participation, exploration, storytelling, art and music children can develop independence, empathy, and respect.

Throughout interaction with other children and our teachers, they gain higher cognitive skills, such as a deep understanding of language, social skills, creativity, and critical thinking.

By being allowed to follow their own interests, children develop curiosity, a passion for learning and a growth mindset.

Our Team

We are a team of educators, parents, nerds, and foodies who believe that the approach to education in Thailand needs to be expanded – especially for children whose parents want a meaningful education that is based on our knowledge of learning and development in the 21st century.

In 2023 we decided to start our work to bring a new bilingual learning program to Bengaluru, an education that is based on the principles of the original German Kindergarten (literally Garden for Children) and puts the child’s interest at the core of its pedagogy.

Sprouts Family

Sprouts was founded by Sprouts Learning Co. Ltd. Our mission is to make learning better, more meaningful and more accessible. Besides operating the Sprouts Childcare Center, we focus on combining technology with social learning experiences. Other projects include the popular Sprouts YouTube Channel, the online skill school Try Fail Do, and the Mali App for New Moms. Read more about us at www.sproutslearning.org
